Frequently Asked Questions:

What is a Product Manager?

A Product Manager works to guide products (websites, web apps, mobile apps, etc) from conception to launch. We bridge the technical and business worlds and create products that users love. I personally work on designing and building responsive web apps.

What is a responsive web app?

When you visit Facebook or Yelp or Gmail on a web browser, instead of opening the mobile app, you just visited a web app. Web apps are different from websites in the sense that they are dynamic and users interact with them. As opposed to websites, like this one, which are primarily informational.
Responsive just means that the web app is optimized to be viewed across different screen sizes, like a tablet and a phone and a computer screen.

Who are your clients?

My clients range from individuals to Fortune 500 companies. I work with them on launching new products and improving on their existing products.

How much do you charge?

I charge either an hourly or per project rate, depending on my client's needs. Since I run my own business, I'm able to work with my clients to find a solution that fits their budget.
